Hotdogs Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 hot dog buns
  • 4 hot dogs

Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Place buns in a single layer in the air fryer basket
  3. cook in the preheated air fryer until crisp, about 2 minutes. Remove buns to a plate.
  4. Place hot dogs in a single layer in the air fryer basket; cook for 3 minutes.
  5. Serve hot dogs in toasted buns.
